The Solution

An AI that doesn't just track what kids get right or wrong — it understands how they think, adapts in real-time, coaches parents, connects peers, and handles compliance automatically.

Print. Solve. Scan. Learn.

  • Teacher generates personalized PDFs per student (based on mastery levels)
  • 6 layout types: standard, grid, two-column, passage, matching, fill-blank
  • Student fills in on paper with pencil
  • Teacher scans with phone camera (batch: whole class stack)
  • AI extracts handwritten answers
  • Handles math equivalence (5/4 = 1 1/4 = 1.25)
  • Flags low-confidence items for teacher review
  • Scores feed back into mastery + learning path

Compliance Built In

FERPA

  • All student PII encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Role-based access (district, school, teacher, guardian, student)
  • Full audit trail on every data access
  • Data deletion support (right to erasure)

COPPA

  • Guardian consent wizard before any child data collection
  • Minimal PII (no email/phone for students)
  • No third-party tracking or advertising
  • Collaboration features require explicit parent opt-in

State Homeschool Laws (All 50 States)

  • Auto-tracks instruction hours from session data
  • Auto-tracks subject coverage from activities
  • Alerts 30 days before state deadlines
  • Auto-generates portfolio PDFs for reviews
  • Pre-fills notification forms for districts

Roles

  • District admin: all data within their district
  • Teacher: own classroom students only
  • Guardian: linked children only
  • Student: own data, read-only + POST interactions
